Our Mission

"At Resala, our mission is clear: to cultivate a culture of giving and generosity while addressing the pressing needs of our society. Through our diverse programs spanning education, healthcare, and social welfare, we aim to create a more equitable and inclusive Egypt. By fostering empathy, collaboration, and sustainable solutions, we aspire to build a brighter future for generations to come, one filled with dignity, opportunity, and compassion."


About Us

"Resala is a beacon of hope in Egypt, dedicated to serving the less fortunate and marginalized communities across the nation. Established with the ethos of compassion and solidarity, we strive to uplift lives, alleviate suffering, and promote social justice. With unwavering commitment and a network of dedicated volunteers, Resala stands as a symbol of community-driven change, offering support, empowerment, and opportunity to those in need."


Impact Metrics

Beneficiaries Helped

Over 100,000 individuals have been positively impacted by our programs and services. We strive to reach even more people in need.

Funds Raised

Millions of dollars have been raised to finance our charitable initiatives. These funds enable us to expand our reach and support more communities.

Projects Completed

More than 500 projects have been successfully completed across various communities. These projects have made a tangible difference in the lives of countless individuals.
