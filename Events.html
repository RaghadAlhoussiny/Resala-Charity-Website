Our Events

"Discover the latest happenings and upcoming events at Resala, where we are dedicated to organizing meaningful gatherings that inspire positive change and make a difference in people's lives"

The Annual Charity Gala:

Our flagship event of the year, the Annual Charity Gala brings together donors, volunteers, and supporters for an unforgettable evening of giving. Enjoy live entertainment, auctions, and inspiring speeches while contributing to Resala's various projects.

Health Awareness Campaigns:

Your health matters to us. Join our health awareness campaigns aimed at promoting wellness and disease prevention in communities across Egypt. From free medical check-ups to educational seminars, we're here to support your well-being.


Children's Day Celebration:

Let's celebrate the joy and innocence of childhood! Join us for a day filled with fun activities, games, and surprises for children from disadvantaged backgrounds. Together, we can bring smiles to their faces and create lasting memories.


Environmental Cleanup Campaigns:

Committed to environmental sustainability, Resala organizes environmental cleanup campaigns to beautify public spaces and raise awareness about the importance of preserving our planet. Volunteers come together to clean beaches, parks, and streets, promoting a cleaner, greener environment for all.
