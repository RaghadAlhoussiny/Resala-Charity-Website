Success stories

At Resala, we believe in the power of compassion and community to transform lives. Through our various initiatives and dedicated volunteers, we've been able to make a tangible difference in the lives of countless individuals across Egypt. Here are some inspiring success stories that demonstrate the impact of our work:

Empowering Women Through Education

Nadia, a single mother from a marginalized community, achieved her dream of higher education through Resala's scholarship program. She enrolled in a vocational training course and excelled in tailoring. With the support of Resala mentors, Nadia now owns a thriving tailoring business, empowering other women in her community to pursue their aspirations.



Building Brighter Futures for Orphans

Fatima and Ali, orphaned at a young age, found solace and support at Resala's orphanage. Through a nurturing environment, they healed from their past traumas and excelled academically and emotionally. With the guidance of Resala's mentors, they have grown into confident young adults with promising futures.



Responding to Crisis with Compassion

During times of crisis, such as natural disasters or economic downturns, Resala has been at the forefront of relief efforts, providing essential aid and support to affected communities. Through the generosity of donors and the dedication of volunteers, Resala has been able to offer food, shelter, and medical assistance to those in need, offering hope and solidarity in their darkest hours.



Empowering Persons with Disabilities Through Vocational Training

Ahmed, a young man with a physical disability, faced discrimination and limited job opportunities. He sought help from Resala's vocational training center for people with disabilities. Through tailored programs, Ahmed gained skills in computer programming and graphic design. With Resala's support, he got a fulfilling job at a local tech company, breaking stereotypes and inspiring his community.
