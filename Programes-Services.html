Programs and Services

"Welcome to Resala, where our diverse range of programs and services aim to make a positive impact in the lives of those in need."

Clothing Drives

Through clothing drives, Resala collects and distributes clothes to disadvantaged communities, including refugees, impoverished families, and homeless individuals. These initiatives help address the basic clothing needs of vulnerable populations.

Ramadan Iftar Programs

During the holy month of Ramadan, Resala hosts iftar programs where volunteers distribute meals to fasting individuals and families in need. These programs promote solidarity and provide relief to those facing food insecurity during Ramadan.

Special Abilities Activities

Are events and programs tailored to support individuals with disabilities, aiming to enhance their quality of life, promote their inclusion in society, and provide them with opportunities for personal development and growth.

Educational Workshops

The organization hosts educational workshops covering a range of topics, including literacy, vocational skills, and financial management. These workshops aim to empower individuals with knowledge and skills for personal and professional development.
